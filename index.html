Contributions

Art to Heart

Art to Heart is a student led non-profit organization dedicated to making a positive impact on underserved communities by providing them with essential art experiences. We host collection and donation drives near parks, in front of supermarkets, and at our schools and have donated to many schools in countries including Cuba, Tibet, Columbia, Angola, and South Africa.

XuanCaiGongYi

XuanCaiGongYi is a non-profit organization that I founded in July 2017. It is an organization through which volunteers help children from underprivileged and impoverished regions in China with their art learning by providing them with school supplies, such as pencils, notebooks, backpacks, and art supplies. So far, more than 15,000 students have benefited from Xuancai Gongyi.

Art Gallery

I started taking art lessons ever since I was five at an art studio in Beijing. I then studied with a Russian artist for three years in Seattle, spent two more years studying art at GW Bellevue, and have then been learning at Studio JHL. My art style covers watercolor, acrylic, oil paint, ceramics, and public art. This section displays some of my artistic pieces.

Public Art

I have been working on public are in the Seattle area for about 3 years now, including painting the public transportation bus stop. My Goal is to bring joy into people's lives by painting positive images that can brighten their day, and giving them a feeling of warmth and community in their life.
