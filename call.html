Calling

  1. Home
  2. Calling

Through the interaction of mixed materials and video combinations, I used an abstract visual language to express my inner feelings: In this interconnected world, I often see many people trapped in many difficulties including poverty. However, there is always a “me” or “you” who exists like a "light." Reaching out of the darkness is a hand that can offer the warmth and guidance. This lets me see their smiles emerge from the warmth of help. Each instance of offering help is also a “calling” from within me, "calling" for a world filled with “love” under the sunlight.
